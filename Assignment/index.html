The Royal Navy Throughout the 18th Century


How Did the Royal Navy Become Established?

When the Kingdom of Great Britain was created by the Acts of Union in 1707, the Royal Nacy was established. This made possible by the merger of the Royal Navy of England and the three-ship Royal Scots Navy. This made the Royal Navy the largest maritime force in the world.
