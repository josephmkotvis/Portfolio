Seven Years War

The strength of England's military, imperial and economic power was unprecidented during this time. At the height of the Seven YEars War in 1759, the Navy had increased to about 300 ships with over 80,000 Navy personnel. This war involved all the great powers of Europe. All the great powers had imperial ambitions which created the tension. Britian won the commercial war, and its navy played a crucial role in defeating the French in Canada. That was were the most important battle happened. Then the Treaty of Paris brought the Seven Years War to an end in 1763.
